Growth Mindset Survey

Have you ever wondered whether you have a growth mindset? We have put together a fun, simple survey to help you determine your current mindset.The goal of this survey is to measure your beliefs about the malleability of intelligence, the relative importance of learning and perfect performance, and your attitudes toward effort and mistakes. At the end of the survey, we have also included some helpful resources for cultivating a growth mindset. Our survey, created for adults and teens, consists of 15 questions.

Your data may be used for a research, and your participation in the research study is voluntary. If you decide to participate in the research survey, please confirm participation by clicking on the "agree" button. If you do not wish to participate in the research study, please decline participation by clicking on the "disagree" button.





This survey was adapted from a questionnaire that Lucinda Poole designed based on Carol Dweck’s book “Mindset: The new psychology of success”(Dweck, 2006).
